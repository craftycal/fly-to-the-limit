Pilots

Fly to the Limit

Fly to the Limit is a small adventure and tour airland operating around the Otago area. We offer tourists a range of scenic flights in both fixed wing and helicopters, as well as high adrenaline thrills in our stunt plane, or the most peaceful flight experience possible in a glider high above the mountains. We specialize in one-off charters, and delivery and pickup for trampers, also providing a provisioning service for fresh supplies during long excursions.

The company is owned by Southland born and bred husband and wife team, Olga and Bruce McKenzie. Travelled extensively, both have a commercial pilot's licenses with 19 and 9 years experience flying for Middle eastern airlines. They own and operate a Cessna Caravan Amphibian which can take up to 7 people to any remote lake in the area. They also own a DG Flugzeugbau DG-1001S Glider with a Cessna Turbo Stationair to tow it. The Cessna is also available for custom scenic tours.
